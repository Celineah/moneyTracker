## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Spreadsheet Layout
The UI SHALL display expenses in a modern card-based format instead of the legacy spreadsheet tabular layout.

#### Scenario: List rendering
- **WHEN** multiple expenses are present
- **THEN** they are displayed as individual cards with rounded corners, soft shadows, and pastel category indicators.

### Requirement: Remaining Money Display
The UI SHALL display a "Remaining Money" section as a prominent Hero Card at the top of the Home screen.

#### Scenario: Display balance
- **WHEN** the Home screen is rendered
- **THEN** the remaining balance is clearly visible within a high-impact card at the top of the view, using elegant typography and theme colors.
